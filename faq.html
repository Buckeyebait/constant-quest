Before you book

The nonresident elk license, plainly.

Wyoming Game and Fish runs a lottery for nonresident elk licenses, and the application window is short. You apply in January, and the online form takes about ten minutes. A preference point bought ahead of time greatly improves your draw odds, so it pays to plan a season or two out.

If you book a hunt with us, you are not on your own for any of this. In January we contact every booked hunter to walk through the application step by step.
