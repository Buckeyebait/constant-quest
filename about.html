Nick Trizano, owner

The owner

Run by someone who has been the guest.

Nick Trizano owns Constant Quest Outfitters. Before he ever owned an outfit, he hunted and fished with many outfits around the world. He knows what a trip feels like from the guest's side of the saddle, the good and the bad of it, and he built this operation around the difference.

The aim is simple. To be the premiere wilderness outfit in Wyoming, and to send every guest home a better hunter, fisherman, or cowboy than the day they arrived. That is the standard we hold the camp, the stock, and the crew to.

The country

Some of the wildest land left in the lower 48.

Our country spans the Thorofare River wilderness, the edge of Yellowstone National Park, the South Fork of the Shoshone, and the Bridger-Teton National Forest. It is big, quiet land that asks something of you and gives plenty back.

Cody sits at 5,016 feet, and from there the trail climbs into high mountain country where the elk live and the rivers run cold and clear. We guide it on horseback the way it has always been done, on land we are proud to be a permittee of.
