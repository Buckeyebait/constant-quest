Constant Quest Outfitters

A genuine wilderness outfit, run the old way.

We are a family run operation out of Cody, Wyoming, guiding quality elk hunts in the famed Thorofare country of the Bridger-Teton and Shoshone National Forest. Our camp sits 26 miles deep in the backcountry, on the banks of the Thorofare River, in what is considered the most remote area in the lower 48 states.

Everything here is done on horseback and on foot, 100% fair chase. We take a limited number of hunters each season so the country, and the quality of the game, stays exactly the way it should be.

Life in camp

Wall tents, wood stoves, and a hot meal waiting.

The ride in is every bit of 7.5 hours and 26 miles. Once you reach camp, you can expect padded cots in wall tents with stoves, and hot meals served in the dining tent each morning and evening, with a packed lunch in the field.

Each day you will be on horseback a minimum of three to four hours with a few miles of walking. Quality stock and comfortable, safe wilderness accommodations are part of the deal.

WYO Wild Horse Ranch

Every trip begins and ends at the ranch.

Fifty miles west of Cody, at the end of the road on the South Fork of the Shoshone, the WYO Wild Horse Ranch is our basecamp. First class cabins, a luxury lodge, the Dizzy Grizzly Saloon, and more than four miles of private river access.

The land has been ranched for over 100 years. Its two homesteads, the Flying H and the historic Lazy Bar F, were both settled in 1916.
